Build Table from Financial Tracking Database
Are you looking for information regarding contributions to specific emergencies, countries, organisations or sectors or would you like to analyse funding by donor?. Then use our contributions search tool. With this function you can generate your own custom-made tables of humanitarian funding by selecting the parameters that you are interested in. You can export the search results into excel. You also have the possiblity to save your query or export it into XML format.
Are you looking for information regarding contributions to specific projects in humanitarian appeals only, then use our project search tool. With this function you can generate your own custom-made tables of humanitarian funding to appeal projects, selecting the data you want from FTS database. You can choose to limit to certain project countries, years, appealing organisations, appeals, clusters or sectors. After generating the table, you will be able to click on the hyperlink in the project codes to read the full project description. You will also be able to download your data in separate spreadsheet.
